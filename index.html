Tokenomics

The $LNQ Token

The $LNQ token, built on the Ethereum blockchain as an ERC-20 token, will be a key component of our ecosystem. The total supply will be 1 billion (1,000,000,000) $LNQ tokens, providing ample liquidity for our users and investors.

Subscription Revenue Allocation

Process

  • A designated percentage of the subscription fee is directed into a dedicated fiat/crypto reserve.
  • At the end of every month, the collected funds in this reserve are used to perform a market buy of the $LNQ token.
  • The acquired tokens are then airdropped to all token holders (100,000 $LNQ minimum required) based on their holding percentage. The greater the amount of $LNQ tokens a user holds, the larger their share from the airdrop.

Unlocking Premium Features

  • Token holders receive enhanced capabilities within the LinqAI SaaS platform.
  • A tier-based approach will be introduced: basic token holders receive a set of features, while higher-tier holders (based on token quantity) access more advanced functionalities.
  • Access to private social channels with the team1

Rewards & Airdrops

  • Regular community events where token holders have the opportunity to earn extra $LNQ tokens.
  • Special promotions, partnerships, or milestones will trigger additional airdrop events.

Subscription Discounts

  • Token holders receive sliding-scale discounts on subscription fees.
  • The discount rate is directly proportional to the number of tokens held. For instance:
    • 100,000 tokens yield a 5% discount.
    • 500,000 tokens provide a 10% discount
    • 1,000,000 tokens provide a 15% discount

Voting Rights

  • Token holders are actively involved in the product development process, the more tokens the more clout (100,000 $LNQ minimum required amount to vote)
  • Periodic polls or surveys will be initiated, allowing stakeholders to express their opinions on various aspects like new features, partnerships, or strategic decisions.
  • There will be tiered groups based on the number of tokens that will allow holders to be rewarded in line with their token holding amount

Early Access Privilege

  • A first-hand experience for token holders, granting them access to alpha/beta versions or novel product launches.
  • These token whales (over 2m $LINQ) will have private social channel and will have a direct line to the team 24/7
  • This not only makes them early adopters but also allows them to provide invaluable feedback for further refinements.
